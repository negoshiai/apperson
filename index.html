A Legacy of Service & Quality

Founded in 1994 by Doug Apperson, a proud Marine Corps veteran, Apperson Utility Contracting, Inc. was built on a foundation of discipline, integrity, and extensive experience with heavy equipment.

As a veteran-owned small business, we bring a unique commitment to every project. We are dedicated to completing our work safely, on time, and on budget, serving communities throughout Central Missouri with unwavering quality.
